  • How the donations would be used

    Here are some examples of our financial needs:

    1. Outreach events.
    2. Computers, office, musical and technical equipment.
    3. Resources, promotion and material costs of developing more ministries to serve students, youth at risk, children and orphans, human trafficking victims, and those suffering under social injustice, to name just a few of our pioneering efforts.
    4. Outreach vehicles.
    5. We would like to purchase office and meeting space.
    6. To support the vision to have other new missional communities pioneered throughout Tohoku and Japan.
    7. To help support Japanese people to join YWAM. There is not yet a strong church culture to financially support young missionaries in Japan, and this is a large barrier for many Japanese to join the mission field.
